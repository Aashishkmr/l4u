Know about the most simple and basic of women safety device below:

  • It is legal to keep pepper spray with you under section 96 and 106 of IPC
  • It is not allowed in cabin luggage at airport

  • It is legal to keep pepper spray with you under section 96 and 106 of IPC
  • It is not allowed in cabin luggage at airport
  • Buy small concealable size to keep in purse or pocket
  • Dont misuse pepper spray.
  • Best way to use pepper spray are listed below
  • Use it from a distance atleast one arm length so that it does not hurt you.
  • It has multiple shots so it can cover multiple threats at same/different time.
  • Practice it so that you are familiar with it that you can use it quickly at adverse times without even looking at nozzle(Dont actually spray but enact).
  • While using, grip it hard through fingers and spray through thumb.
  • Keep scanning the areas and keep pepper spray armed/unlocked when you see potential thret situations.
  • Only disarm when reach a safe place.
  • Target the eyes of attacker.
  • Dont use expired pepper spray(because cannister will loose pressure)
  • While buying be known about spray distance, spray pattern, number of sprays
  • Differnt spray pattern:
    -Stream patterns give good range but require aiming directly at the face(best: ear to ear).
    -Cone mists have shorter range but you don't have to aim as well just spray up and down around face. But if there is a breeze, a stream is more controllable. Mists can be blown off target or even back to the sprayer.
  • Step on the side after spraying rather than going back because a blinder attacker will inherently attack in front of himself
  • Keep opposite arm outsrecthed to push him if he comes on to you
